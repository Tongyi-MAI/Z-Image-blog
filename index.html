Introduction

Welcome to the official repository for the Z-Image project! This is your central hub for everything related to the Z-Image model and its core technologies. We are pleased to introduce Z-Image, an efficient 6-billion-parameter foundation model for image generation. It demonstrates that through systematic optimization, state-of-the-art performance can be achieved with a moderately sized model and reasonable resource consumption. Z-Image shows strong results in photorealistic image generation and bilingual text rendering, with quality comparable to leading commercial models.
